{% trans %}

You can use projects to create and manage collections of data packages.

Projects can contain packages from different organizations.

A project can for example represent a research project, a PhD thesis, or a cross-cutting topic.

Projects are just another way to help people find what they are looking for.

Everybody can create a project and become project administrator who can add and remove data packes to and from her project.
