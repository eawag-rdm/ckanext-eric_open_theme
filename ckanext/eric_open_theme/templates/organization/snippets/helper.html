Organizations correspond to Eawag departments and research groups.

Each data package is associated with a research group or a department.

Authorization to create, edit, delete and view data packages is managed on the level of organizations.

Data Managers have administrative rights for their respective organization(s) and can

  • assign organization membership
  • assign permissions to users
  • modify the organization description
